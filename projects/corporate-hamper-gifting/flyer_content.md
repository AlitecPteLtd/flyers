# Flyer Content — Corporate Hamper Gifting

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Corporate Hamper Gifting

**Subtitle:** Multi-Recipient Delivery — eCommerce, Counter Sales & Gifting with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's corporate gifting solution lets customers buy many hampers in one checkout — each with its own delivery address, shipping date, and greeting message — while your team fulfils, invoices, and delivers from one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **One Checkout, Many Gifts** — Assign a different recipient and date to every hamper line.
2. **Greeting Messages** — Card recipient, sender, and greeting carried to fulfilment.
3. **Counter Quick Sale** — Cash-and-carry roadshow flow with receipt printing and pricelists.
4. **Smart Delivery Fees** — Shipping costs calculated per address and hamper quantity.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Multi-Address Checkout** — Website cart with per-line delivery recipient and date.
2. **Greeting Card Fields** — Recipient, sender, greeting, and delivery note on each gift.
3. **Greeting Templates** — Pre-fill message templates for faster online ordering.
4. **Pricelist Control** — Default pricelists for counter quick-sale and standard sales.
5. **Counter Quick Sale** — Roadshow orders with cash-and-carry and payment journals.
6. **Pickup Options** — Cash & carry, self collection, or ship-to-recipient delivery.
7. **Split Fulfilment** — One order generates multiple deliveries by address and date.
8. **Branded Documents** — Custom sales, invoice, and delivery print layouts.

## Business Workflow

1. **Shop or Sell** — Order online or create a counter quick-sale with the right pricelist.
2. **Assign Recipients** — Set delivery address, date, and greeting on each hamper line.
3. **Pay Once** — Single payment with delivery fees grouped by shipment.
4. **Pick & Pack** — Greeting details flow to warehouse moves and delivery slips.
5. **Deliver** — Multiple gift deliveries from one corporate order.

**Control modes:**
- Per-line shipping recipient and shipping date
- Hide or show greeting fields on website
- Blocked delivery dates on checkout
- Reseller vs corporate customer handling
- Team warehouse and payment method defaults

**Additional features:**
- Receipt printing for counter sales
- Barcode-to-delivery wizard
- Multiple terms and conditions
- WhatsApp connector for customer messaging
- Warehouse reporting for fulfilment teams
