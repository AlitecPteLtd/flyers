Complete Storage Rental Operations with Odoo

Alitec's Storage Rental Business solution on Odoo helps self-storage operators manage enquiries, unit availability, rental contracts, portal access, recurring billing, payments, and branch-level operating visibility.

2D Unit Control

Map branches, floors, sections, unit positions, merged units, and availability.

Lead to Rental

Convert CRM enquiries into storage quotations, rental orders, and subscriptions.

Customer Portal

Expose rented products, paid-through dates, invoices, payments, and actions.

Recurring Billing

Support prepayment, deposits, recurring invoices, admin fees, and deferred revenue.
