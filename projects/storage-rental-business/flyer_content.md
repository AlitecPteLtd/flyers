# Flyer Content - Storage Rental Business

## Cover

Title: Storage Rental Business

Subtitle: Unit Inventory, Rental Portal, Billing and Branch Control with Odoo

Intro: Alitec's Storage Rental Business solution on Odoo helps self-storage operators manage the full rental lifecycle, from enquiry and unit availability to rental order, portal access, payment, invoicing, deferred revenue, and branch visibility.

## Quick Benefits

- 2D Unit Control: Map branches, floors, sections, unit positions, merged units, and live availability.
- Lead to Rental: Convert CRM enquiries into storage quotations, rental orders, and subscriptions.
- Customer Portal: Show rented products, paid-through dates, invoices, payments, and customer actions.
- Recurring Billing: Support prepaid rental, recurring invoices, deposits, admin fees, and deferred revenue.

## Key Capabilities

- 2D Unit Map: Visualize branch, floor, section, unit position, status color, and merged/split units.
- Storage Status: Control available, reserved, occupied, blocked, and overlocked storage statuses.
- Unit Type Master: Maintain unit type, size, dimensions, deposit, rental price, and unit features.
- Rental Contracts: Create rental orders with move-in date, period, payment method, and branch.
- Portal Payments: Guide customers through portal invoices, payment methods, outstanding balances, and receipts.
- Revenue Control: Support recurring rental, deposits, invoice sequence, deferred revenue, and migration continuity.

## Workflow

Enquiry -> Unit Match -> Rental Order -> Payment -> Renew / Move Out

## Business Benefits

- Better Unit Visibility: Control branch, floor, section, unit status, and merged-unit availability.
- Faster Lead Conversion: Move enquiries into matched units, quotations, and rental orders faster.
- More Accurate Billing: Invoice deposits, prepaid rental, recurring rental, and deferred revenue from one flow.
- Stronger Coordination: Keep sales, branch teams, portal users, operations, and finance aligned.
