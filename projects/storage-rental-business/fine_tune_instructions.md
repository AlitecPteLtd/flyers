# Fine-Tune Instructions - Storage Rental Business

## Visual Direction

- Use a realistic self-storage image: clean indoor storage corridors, storage units, staff/customer interaction, boxes, trolleys, or reception counter.
- Keep the left side readable with a strong white fade and dark navy title text.
- Keep the Odoo Gold Partner logo on the right side over a clean, low-detail area.
- Avoid embedded text inside generated hero images, because it becomes unreadable after scaling.

## Layout Rules

- Hero image must remain a background layer, not a content block that pushes the page down.
- Do not let the hero wave or curve cover intro text.
- Keep quick-benefit card text fully visible; no clipped lines.
- Keep circles, icons, and labels optically centered.
- Keep May/June graph points aligned to the same x-axis spacing as the other months.

## Content Rules

- Use storage rental terminology, not scaffolding or construction terminology.
- Highlight unit availability, branch/floor/section control, rental contracts, portal payments, and recurring billing.
- Keep copy short enough for A4 print; avoid long paragraphs in capability cards.
- Maintain English and Chinese versions together when updating content.
