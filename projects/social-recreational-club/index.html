Alitec's social and recreational club solution helps membership clubs run enrolment, facility booking, events and banquets, restaurant POS, and levy billing — with a member portal, digital QR cards, and one connected record from signup to accounting.

Member Portal

Facility Booking

Events & Banquets

Registration, group booking, barcode attendance, and proposal documents.

Club F&B POS

Restaurant POS with member ID, split bills, service charges, and table QR.
