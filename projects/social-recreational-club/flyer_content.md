# Flyer Content — Social/Recreational Club

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Social/Recreational Club

**Subtitle:** Membership, Facilities, Events & F&B — Managed with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's social and recreational club solution helps membership clubs run enrolment, facility booking, events and banquets, restaurant POS, and levy billing — with a member portal, digital QR cards, and one connected record from signup to accounting.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Member Portal** — Online signup, digital QR membership card, and self-service account access.
2. **Facility Booking** — Resource-based appointments with capacity, peak pricing, and web booking.
3. **Events & Banquets** — Registration, group booking, barcode attendance, and proposal documents.
4. **Club F&B POS** — Restaurant POS with member ID, split bills, service charges, and table QR.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Multi-Tier Membership** — Student, ordinary, life, social, corporate, and family membership types.
2. **Approval Onboarding** — Membership applications routed through Odoo Approvals before activation.
3. **Digital Member Card** — Portal QR membership card with transaction and levy visibility.
4. **Facility Resources** — Book courts, rooms, and venues with capacity and holiday pricing rules.
5. **Events & Banquets** — Web registration, group members, LOF/LOC workflow, and seminar PDFs.
6. **Restaurant POS** — Split bills, service charges, kitchen printing, and member on receipts.
7. **MSL Billing** — Minimum spending levy calculated from POS consumption by member group.
8. **Club Finance** — Subscriptions, facility invoices, bulk payments, and export reports.

## Business Workflow

1. **Apply Online** — Membership signup with documents, PDPA consent, and payment details.
2. **Approve Member** — Approval workflow activates membership and subscription billing.
3. **Portal Access** — Digital QR card, transactions, MSL view, and helpdesk services.
4. **Book & Register** — Facility slots and event registration with group booking support.
5. **Dine & Bill** — POS identifies members; spend feeds MSL and club accounting.

**Control modes:**
- Member types: student, ordinary, life, social, corporate, family
- Facility capacity, peak charges, unpaid booking timeout
- Banquet proposal, confirmation, sectioned order lines
- MSL groups with minimum spending rules from POS
- Reception desk, visitor check-in, member verification

**Additional features:**
- Promotions and notices CMS for members
- Event barcode attendance and group registration
- Split bills and service charges in restaurant POS
- Table QR ordering for club dining outlets
- Two-factor authentication and password policy
- Banquet, F&B, and facility booking Excel exports
- Facility cancellation loyalty coupons
- Recruitment and member activity reporting
