This customized solution extends Odoo Sales, Purchase, Inventory and Invoicing for scaffolding businesses. It manages rental quotations, customer POs, rental orders, deliveries, returns, repair and lost charges, work done, claims, intercompany warehouse movements, and statement of account generation.

Key Capabilities

1. Rental Order Flow

Quotation, customer PO, rental order, delivery, return, and recurring billing.

2. Delivery & Return Control

Track site dispatches, return balances, repair, loss, and over-return handling.

3. Project Claims

Manage contracts, work done, erection and dismantling records, claims, and certificates.

4. M3 Rental Billing

Support volume-based rental with density, delivery percentage, and monthly billing.

5. New / Used Item Logic

Route stock from new or used locations and post revenue to the correct accounts.

6. Intercompany Warehouse

Mirror pickings across companies and keep project references aligned.
