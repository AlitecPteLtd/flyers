# Fine-Tune Instructions — Aquaculture Lab Testing Process

## Confirmed Business Positioning

Position this as an aquaculture lab operations solution for farm-service businesses — not generic ERP marketing. The differentiator is the end-to-end link between farmer sample intake, five specialized lab test workflows, prescription recommendations, and commercial follow-through in Odoo.

## Flyer Style Direction

- Follow the approved Alitec A4 portrait brochure shell used in other flyer projects.
- Use a blue / cyan aquaculture palette rather than the renovation-style navy only.
- Keep the hero visual farm- and lab-oriented: ponds, samples, aquaculture testing context.
- Preserve the standard Alitec header, curved navy band, capability row, workflow panel, dashboard panel, integrations row, benefits band, and dark footer.

## Approved Title and Subtitle Direction

- **Title:** Lab Testing Process
- **Subtitle:** Aquaculture Testing — From Sample to Solution with Odoo

## Visual Cues to Preserve

- Left text-safe zone over the hero image
- Quiet top-right area for Odoo Gold Partner branding
- Icon-based capability blocks
- Five-step workflow with connected step line
- Dashboard with KPI cards, chart, and mini table
- Six-item footer using `alitec.asia` contacts per workflow standard

## Things to Avoid in Later Revisions

- Do not claim CRM, website, POS, or manufacturing features — they are not in the core custom scope.
- Do not present standard Odoo sales or invoicing as custom innovation.
- Avoid developer terms such as model names, wizards, or inheritance language in customer-facing copy.
- Do not overcrowd the hero image or place logos on busy visual areas.
- Do not create the PPT until the flyer is reviewed and approved.

## Future PPT Stage Instructions

When the user approves the flyer:

- Reuse the same title, subtitle, and business positioning.
- Expand the five-step workflow and five test types into dedicated slides if needed.
- Keep the cyan/blue aquaculture visual language consistent.
- Use the same footer contact details.
- Emphasize farmer sample traceability, auto test creation, parameter control, prescription upsell, and branded reports as the main story arc.
