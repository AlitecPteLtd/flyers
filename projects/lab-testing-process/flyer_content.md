# Flyer Content — Aquaculture Lab Testing Process

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Lab Testing Process

**Subtitle:** Aquaculture Testing — From Sample to Solution with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's lab testing solution helps aquaculture service providers manage farmers, field samples, five test types, treatment recommendations, and branded reports in one Odoo platform — from sample intake to commercial follow-through.

**Quick benefits:**
1. **Farmer-Linked Samples** — Register farmers with district, cluster, farm, and pond context tied to every sample batch.
2. **Auto Lab Creation** — Confirmed sales orders automatically open the correct water, soil, seed, PCR, or microbiology tests.
3. **Range-Based Control** — Maintain parameter libraries with optimum values to support consistent interpretation.
4. **Prescription Upsell** — Convert completed test recommendations into quotation options for treatment products.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Farmer & Sample Registry** — Track farmers, species, membership, and sample batches with farm and pond detail.
2. **Five Lab Test Types** — Run water quality, soil profile, seed quality, PCR diagnostics, and microbiology in dedicated workflows.
3. **Sales-Triggered Testing** — Create lab jobs automatically when test products are sold and the order is confirmed.
4. **Parameter Libraries** — Configure test parameters, optimum ranges, and default panels for faster lab entry.
5. **Plankton & PCR Support** — Handle plankton analysis in water testing and flexible PCR disease panel setup.
6. **Prescription Workflow** — Attach treatment product recommendations to completed tests and push them to the quotation.
7. **Branded Lab Reports** — Produce customer-ready PDF outputs with lab manager and signature support.
8. **Commercial Continuity** — Connect testing activity to sales, inventory, membership, delivery, and invoicing.

## Business Workflow

1. **Register & Sample** — Create the farmer record and collect sample batches from farm and pond locations.
2. **Order Tests** — Build a quotation with the required test products and linked samples.
3. **Run Lab** — Move each test through sample receipt, testing, review, and completion stages.
4. **Recommend Treatments** — Record prescription products based on lab findings where applicable.
5. **Deliver & Bill** — Issue branded reports, convert recommendations to order options, and complete billing.

**Control modes:**
- Farmer-linked sample batches
- Single or multiple sample sales orders
- Six-stage lab state tracking
- Prescription-to-quotation conversion
- Branded combined and individual reports

**Additional features:**
- District and cluster farmer IDs
- Species and batch sample traceability
- Default PCR and microbiology parameter loading
- Lab manager name and signature on reports
- Test counters and quick-open actions from sales orders
