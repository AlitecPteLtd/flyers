# Flyer Content — Outlet Food Manufacturing Operations

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Food Manufacturing Operations

**Subtitle:** Fresh Food & Sushi Production — From Kitchen to Outlet with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's food manufacturing solution helps fresh-food producers plan daily outlet production, generate manufacturing and delivery orders together, enforce outlet-level cost control, and reconcile commission sales in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Produce to Outlet** — Production templates generate manufacturing orders and delivery orders together for each outlet and scheduled date.
2. **Margin by Outlet** — Analytic accounts are required and applied across sales, purchases, production, and invoicing.
3. **Commission Billing** — Excel sales imports create customer invoices and matching commission credit notes by outlet.
4. **Expiry Traceability** — Delivery slips and invoices print lot, serial, and expiration dates for perishable goods.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Outlet Production Templates** — Plan daily production by outlet, location, analytic account, and product quantity.
2. **Bulk MO + Delivery Generation** — Create manufacturing orders and outlet deliveries from one wizard action.
3. **Analytic Accounting Control** — Enforce outlet cost centers on sales, purchases, MOs, invoices, and inventory moves.
4. **Excel Sales Import** — Map third-party sales lines to products and outlets from spreadsheet uploads.
5. **Commission Automation** — Generate customer invoices and commission credit notes from imported sales.
6. **Split Deliveries** — Support per-line delivery addresses and separate pickings by destination.
7. **Lot & Expiry Documents** — Show manufacturing and expiration detail on delivery slips and invoices.
8. **Branded Operational PDFs** — Print sales, purchase, delivery, invoice, receipt, and statement layouts.

## Business Workflow

1. **Configure** — Set up outlets, products, analytic accounts, and production templates.
2. **Produce** — Run “To Produce” for the scheduled production day.
3. **Manufacture & Deliver** — Complete MOs and ship finished goods to outlets.
4. **Sell & Procure** — Process sales and purchases with mandatory analytics and delivery rules.
5. **Reconcile & Bill** — Import third-party sales, post invoices, and print operational documents.
