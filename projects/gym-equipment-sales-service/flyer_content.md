# Flyer Content — Gym Equipment Sales & Service

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Gym Equipment Sales & Service

**Subtitle:** B2B & B2C Distribution — Sales, Delivery & Maintenance with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's gym equipment solution helps distributors sell through B2B and online channels, register installed machines on delivery, run maintenance contracts, and complete signed field service visits — all in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Dual Sales Channels** — Run B2B quotes and B2C web orders from one equipment catalog.
2. **Installed Equipment** — Track every machine by customer, serial number, and site location.
3. **Contract Maintenance** — Schedule recurring visits and batch monthly service orders.
4. **Field Service Docs** — Checklists, parts used, and signed maintenance or repair reports.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Brand & Category Catalog** — Subcategories, brands, dimensions, and footprints on products.
2. **B2B Sales Tools** — Target discounts, delivery contacts, and after-sales quotation types.
3. **eCommerce Storefront** — Online fitness shop with brands, filters, and checkout options.
4. **Equipment Registration** — Register serial-numbered assets when equipment is delivered.
5. **Maintenance Contracts** — Lump sum, per-service, leasing, and renewal quotation flows.
6. **Service Orders** — Maintenance, repair, troubleshooting, commissioning, and shifting.
7. **Service Checklists** — Product-linked templates completed on every service visit.
8. **Signed Service Reports** — Digital signatures on delivery and service PDF documents.

## Business Workflow

1. **Quote & Sell** — B2B quotation or web order with brand catalog and warranties.
2. **Deliver & Register** — Signed delivery, serial capture, push equipment to after-sales.
3. **Open Contract** — Link machines to a maintenance contract with billing and frequency.
4. **Schedule Service** — Generate monthly maintenance or on-demand repair orders.
5. **Complete & Bill** — Checklists, signatures, service PDFs, and follow-up invoices.

**Control modes:**
- Standard vs after-sales sales teams
- Contract billing types (lump sum, per service, leasing)
- Service type routing (maintenance, repair, troubleshooting)
- Warranty codes on equipment lines
- Appointment windows and time-in/time-out on visits

**Additional features:**
- Branded sales, delivery, and invoice print layouts
- Monthly maintenance batch wizard
- Parts replacement list on service orders
- Subscription billing UI support
- Consignment picking linked to sales lines
- CRM and inventory operational dashboards
