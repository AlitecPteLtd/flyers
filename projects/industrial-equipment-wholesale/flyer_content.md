# Flyer Content — Industrial Equipment Wholesale

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Industrial Equipment Wholesale

**Subtitle:** B2B Distribution — Portal Sync, Procurement & Invoicing with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's industrial equipment wholesale solution helps B2B distributors sync portal orders, manage model and part-number catalogs, raise vendor POs from sales lines, track stock and delivery modes, and issue package-aware GST invoices — all in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Portal Order Sync** — Import sales and purchase orders with customers, vendors, and products.
2. **Part-Number Catalog** — Model, part number, serial tracking, and last purchase price on products.
3. **SO to PO** — Create or extend vendor POs directly from sales order lines.
4. **Package Invoices** — Grouped invoice lines with model/part numbers on branded tax PDFs.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Portal/API Sync** — Batch and on-demand import of SO, PO, products, and partners.
2. **Industrial SKU Data** — Model, part number, freight charge, and serial tracking on products.
3. **SO-to-PO Procurement** — Wizard to create vendor POs from sales lines with line-level linkage.
4. **Stock on Sales** — On-hand, committed, incoming, and available qty on the sales order.
5. **Delivery Modes** — Self collection, local delivery, ex-works, and sales-engineer handoff.
6. **Package Invoicing** — Bundle lines on invoices; hide internal lines on customer PDFs.
7. **Branded Documents** — Custom tax invoice and purchase order PDFs with payment terms.
8. **Finance Controls** — Landed costs, analytic per order, and negative-stock prevention.

## Business Workflow

1. **Sync Master Data** — Products, customers, and suppliers imported from external portal.
2. **Receive Sales Order** — SO synced with customer PO ref, shipping note, and analytic account.
3. **Raise Vendor PO** — Create or extend PO from SO lines with line-level traceability.
4. **Receive & Deliver** — Goods receipt, delivery with mode and shipping instructions.
5. **Invoice & Pay** — Package-aware GST invoice, landed costs, and payment modes.

## Business Benefits

1. **One Order Truth** — Portal sync keeps sales, purchase, stock, and accounting aligned.
2. **Faster Quote-to-PO** — Raise vendor POs from SO lines without re-keying.
3. **Cleaner Invoices** — Package-aware PDFs with model and part numbers for B2B buyers.
4. **Controlled Ops** — Landed costs, analytics, and no-negative stock on every move.
