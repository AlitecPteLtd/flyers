# Flyer Content — Precision CNC Manufacturing

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Precision CNC Manufacturing

**Subtitle:** High-Mix Job Shop — Materials, Planning & Billing with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's precision CNC manufacturing solution helps job shops run high-mix, low-volume orders from quotation to shop floor — with operation routing, material tracking, multi-level MRP planning, in-process QC, and billing linked to sales, delivery, and production.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Quote to Floor** — Sales orders spawn MOs with commitment-driven scheduling and live progress.
2. **Operation Control** — Route parts through work centers, process notes, and step-specific QC rules.
3. **Quality to Ship** — In-process checks, shop-floor controls, and Certificate of Conformance on delivery.
4. **Plan with MRP** — Multi-level MRP, warehouse calendars, and hierarchical MO visibility.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Multi-Level MOs** — Parent and child manufacturing orders with hierarchy chart and scheduling.
2. **Operation Routing** — Named processes, work centers, process notes, and sequenced routing steps.
3. **Shop Floor Control** — Work order barcodes, timers, assignments, and gated partial completion.
4. **Per-Step QC Rules** — Quality checks linked to operations with scrap and alert traceability.
5. **Material Tracking** — Stock by location, BoM consumption, PO balance, and component warnings.
6. **MRP Planning** — Multi-level scheduler, planned orders, and warehouse calendar lead times.
7. **CoC & Travelers** — MO travelers, BoM overview, and Certificate of Conformance on delivery.
8. **Sell to Bill** — SO, MO, delivery, and invoice traceability with branded document pack.

## Business Workflow

1. **Win the Job** — Quotation and SO with commitment dates, credit check, and QMS references.
2. **Engineer & Plan** — BoM routing, MRP scheduler, MO planning status, and schedule dates.
3. **Release to Floor** — Work orders with barcodes, QC rules, process notes, and assignments.
4. **Make & Inspect** — Shop-floor execution, per-operation QC, and controlled partial completion.
5. **Ship & Bill** — Delivery with CoC, barcode labels, and invoice linked to SO and DO.

**Control modes:**
- Parent and child MO scheduling with days-to-produce rollup
- Pending RM, assembly, and ready states on work orders
- Operation-linked quality rules and supervisor partial-qty control
- Back-scheduling from customer line commitment dates
- Part numbers, lot dates, and barcode labels on delivery

**Additional features:**
- Branded MO travelers and CoC PDF layouts
- BoM structure export and change audit trail
- Planned vs actual minutes per operation
- Direct and indirect purchase order types
- MO progress percent on sales orders
- Multi-level MO hierarchy navigator
- Smart warnings on sales and manufacturing documents
- Manufacturing timesheet on work orders
