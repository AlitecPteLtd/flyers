# Flyer Content — Specialty Chemical Trading

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Specialty Chemical Trading

**Subtitle:** Regional Distribution — Sales, Import & Compliance with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's chemical trading solution helps distributors manage indent and ex-stock sales, margin-controlled quotations, batch traceability, landed import costs, and multi-level approvals — across sales, warehouse, and finance in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Trade Order Control** — Indent and ex-stock flows with ports, incoterms, and shipment terms.
2. **Margin Governance** — VOC and minimum price checks before quotations are released.
3. **Batch Traceability** — Lots, shelf life, and expiration on receipts, deliveries, and invoices.
4. **Approval Workflows** — Tier validation on sales, purchases, stock moves, and invoices.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Chemical Product Catalog** — COO, manufacturer, business unit, and strategic BU on products.
2. **Customer Trade Profiles** — Grading, segments, credit limits, and trade vs non-trade routing.
3. **Indent & Ex-Stock Sales** — Order types with RCC partners, agency fees, and shipping data.
4. **Price & Margin Approval** — VOC, min selling price, and blocked quote send until approved.
5. **Shelf-Life Enforcement** — GR validation when batch shelf-life conflicts are detected.
6. **Landed Cost Allocation** — Import cost splits by business unit onto chemical batches.
7. **Branded Trade Documents** — Invoices with lot/expiration lines, delivery notes, and statements.
8. **Website Quote Requests** — eCommerce catalog with grouped products and quote checkout.

## Business Workflow

1. **Quote & Approve** — Build quotation with margin checks and tier validation sign-off.
2. **Order & Source** — Confirm indent purchase or ex-stock allocation with shipping terms.
3. **Receive & Cost** — Goods receipt with shelf-life checks and landed cost posting.
4. **Deliver & Invoice** — Ship with lot traceability and print branded trade invoices.
5. **Monitor & Report** — Track credit, slow movers, commissions, and AR aging.

**Control modes:**
- Indent vs ex-stock order routing
- Trade / non-trade and agency partner types
- Customer grading and available credit limit
- Slow-moving stock controls on sales and purchase
- Multi-company analytic distribution on lines

**Additional features:**
- Contact approval before use on transactions
- Split deliveries by line delivery dates
- Sales contract generation from DOCX templates
- Purchase-from-sale for indent sourcing
- Excel reports for trade interest, VAT, and lead time
