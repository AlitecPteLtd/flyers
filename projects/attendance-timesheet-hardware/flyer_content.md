## Subtitle

Biometric capture, daily in/out processing, Excel payroll exports, and site-ready timesheet control

## Intro Paragraph

Alitec's attendance and timesheet solution on Odoo helps operations teams bring biometric punch data, employee attendance control, and team-based timesheet work into one managed workflow. It covers ZKTeco device connectivity, employee code matching, daily in/out processing, shift-aware working-hour calculation, manual review, Excel exports, and imported site timesheets for approval.

## Quick Benefits

1. Direct device sync
   Bring biometric logs into Odoo with device, area, and connection visibility.

2. Daily attendance control
   Build one simple attendance table with in/out time, status, and device traceability.

3. Shift-aware processing
   Split day and night hours, preserve manual overrides, and support missing-punch review.

4. Timesheet-ready operations
   Import worker sheets from Excel or CSV and move them through confirmation and approval.
