# Fine-Tune Instructions

## Confirmed Positioning

Position this project as an Odoo attendance and timesheet solution with biometric hardware integration.

## Scope To Keep

- ZKTeco biometric device connection and device control
- employee code mapping
- attendance log processing
- simple attendance daily records
- day and night shift handling
- Excel attendance exports
- team-based or site-based timesheet import and approval

## Scope To Exclude

- do not describe the OCR or PDF timesheet-processing modules
- do not lead with generic payroll marketing
- do not overclaim that every timesheet action is automatically generated from biometric logs

## Title Direction

Use:

- `Odoo Attendance & Timesheet with Hardware`

Subtitle direction:

- biometric capture
- daily in/out control
- payroll-ready exports
- site timesheet operations

## Visual Direction

- use a clean white corporate flyer shell
- keep Alitec on the left and Odoo Gold Partner on the top right
- prefer a right-side operational visual panel instead of a crowded stock photo
- show device, attendance, and timesheet cues in the hero area
- keep key capability boxes balanced with consistent margins

## Things To Avoid

- overlapping sections
- month labels that do not match chart points
- footer or benefits bands colliding with the integration row
- unreadable dense text inside colored bars
- oversized hero visuals that push text out of balance

## HTML Editing Rule

Keep headline, subtitle, box titles, body paragraphs, app names, benefits, and footer text editable in the browser using the shared flyer editor script.
