# Flyer Content — Secondary Process Manufacturing

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Secondary Process Manufacturing

**Subtitle:** Incoming Jobs, IQC/OQC & Shop Floor Control with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's manufacturing solution helps secondary-process factories receive customer materials, run traveler-based production jobs, control IQC and OQC quality gates, merge and split shop orders, and track every job from intake to release in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Traveler Routing** — Customer parts drive production through traveler templates and phased routings.
2. **IQC / OQC Gates** — Incoming and outgoing quality phases with reject, rework, and engineering holds.
3. **Merge Shop Orders** — Combine manufacturing orders at the next shared ready operation.
4. **Full Job Tracking** — Split, combine, serial, and lineage tracking across production jobs.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Traveler Templates** — Link customer parts to internal BoM and routing templates.
2. **Incoming Material Jobs** — Register received parts and launch manufacturing from material intake.
3. **IQC & OQC Phases** — Tag work centers for incoming and outgoing quality checkpoints.
4. **Split Production Lots** — Split quantities while preserving work order and check history.
5. **Merge Manufacturing Orders** — Batch orders at the same ready shop-floor operation.
6. **Rework & Reject Flow** — Route rejected quantity to repair, engineering, and quality stations.
7. **Serial & Lineage Track** — Register serials and trace split, combine, and origin MO links.
8. **Compliance Documentation** — Specs, drawings, quality records, and barcoded job travelers for release.

## Business Workflow

1. **Receive Material** — Log incoming customer parts with specs and traveler mapping.
2. **Create Job** — Launch manufacturing from customer product to traveler template routing.
3. **Run Operations** — Execute process stations on the shop floor with work orders.
4. **IQC / OQC Checks** — Complete quality checks; reject or rework nonconforming quantity.
5. **Release & Return** — Finish jobs, document quality results, and return to customer.

**Control modes:**
- IQC / Process / OQC work center phases
- Traveler template routing per customer part
- Combined-pending state while batch MO is active
- Engineering hold and repair order routing
- IORA customer-agreement on IQC rejection

**Additional features:**
- Product specs, PDFs, material, and treatment master data
- Rework inserted before OQC in shop-floor sequence
- Shop-floor split and combine actions
- Barcoded manufacturing traveler printouts
- Engineering-done release from quality hold
