Incoming Jobs, IQC/OQC & Shop Floor Control with Odoo

Alitec's manufacturing solution helps secondary-process factories receive customer materials, run traveler-based production jobs, control IQC and OQC quality gates, merge and split shop orders, and track every job from intake to release in one Odoo platform.

Traveler Routing

Customer parts drive production through traveler templates and phased routings.

IQC / OQC Gates

Incoming and outgoing quality phases with reject, rework, and engineering holds.

Merge Shop Orders

Combine manufacturing orders at the next shared ready shop-floor operation.

Full Job Tracking

Split, combine, serial, and lineage tracking across every production job.
