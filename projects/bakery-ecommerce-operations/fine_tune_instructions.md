# Fine-Tune Instructions — Bakery E-Commerce & Production Control

## Confirmed Business Positioning

Position this as a bakery e-commerce and production-control solution for cake and patisserie businesses with online ordering, POS loyalty, daily capacity limits, and fulfillment scheduling. The differentiator is preventing overselling while coordinating delivery, collection, kitchen reports, and membership rewards.

## Flyer Style Direction

- Follow the approved Alitec A4 portrait brochure shell.
- Use a warm bakery palette with cream, rose, and gold accents while keeping the standard navy header/footer shell.
- Hero should show cakes, cookies, and patisserie products clearly on the right with text-safe space on the left.
- Use the fading navy curve stripe, not a solid blocking band.
- Apply the standard `720px` quick-benefits layout with icon-left / heading-right rows.

## Approved Title and Subtitle Direction

- **Title:** Bakery E-Commerce Operations
- **Subtitle:** Cakes & Cookies Online — From Web Shop to Kitchen with Odoo

## Visual Cues to Preserve

- Left text-safe hero overlay
- Quiet top-right Odoo Gold Partner branding
- Eight capability icons, five-step workflow, dashboard panel, integrations row, benefits band
- Six-item `alitec.asia` footer

## Things to Avoid in Later Revisions

- Do not claim full MRP, BOM management, recipe costing, or automated manufacturing scheduling.
- Do not claim a dedicated 3D cake designer unless such a module is added.
- Do not overstate POS scope beyond loyalty and membership controls evidenced in code.
- Do not use repo or client internal names in customer-facing copy unless requested.
- Do not create the PPT until the flyer is reviewed and approved.

## Future PPT Stage Instructions

When the user approves the flyer:

- Lead with online ordering + daily capacity control.
- Add separate emphasis slides for delivery/collection scheduling and membership/loyalty if needed.
- Keep kitchen and fulfillment reporting as the operations slide.
- Reuse the warm bakery visual language and standard footer contacts.
