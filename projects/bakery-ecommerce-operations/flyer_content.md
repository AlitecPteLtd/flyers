# Flyer Content — Bakery E-Commerce & Production Control

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Bakery E-Commerce Operations

**Subtitle:** Cakes & Cookies Online — From Web Shop to Kitchen with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's bakery solution helps cake and patisserie businesses sell online and in-store with daily production capacity control, delivery and collection scheduling, customized product options, and member loyalty in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Never Oversell Production** — Category daily maximums block orders when the kitchen is already full.
2. **Smart Delivery Slots** — Delivery, collection, holidays, and delicate-product time rules stay aligned.
3. **Members Stay Loyal** — Paid memberships auto-issue coupons and tiered pricing online and in-store.
4. **Kitchen-Ready Fulfillment** — Daily kitchen lists and delivery or collection reports drive production.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Online Bakery Shop** — Sell cakes and patisserie products with stock-aware e-commerce.
2. **Delivery or Collection** — Support home delivery and outlet self-collection with carrier rules.
3. **Date & Time Booking** — Let customers choose fulfillment dates and time slots at checkout.
4. **Daily Capacity Control** — Manage production limits by product category and order date.
5. **Holiday & Blocked Dates** — Stop orders on blackout days and full-capacity dates.
6. **Custom Product Options** — Handle variants and product options for customized cake orders.
7. **Membership & Coupons** — Issue annual or lifetime membership rewards automatically.
8. **Kitchen & Fulfillment Reports** — Export kitchen lists and delivery or collection run sheets.

## Business Workflow

1. **Shop & Customize** — Logged-in customers choose products, variants, and options online.
2. **Schedule Fulfillment** — Pick delivery or collection, date, and time slot.
3. **Pay Securely** — Checkout validates capacity before payment completes.
4. **Produce & Dispatch** — Kitchen lists and fulfillment reports drive daily production.
5. **Retain Members** — Membership perks, coupons, and birthday offers bring customers back.

**Control modes:**
- Category-based daily production caps
