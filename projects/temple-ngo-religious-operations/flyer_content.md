# Flyer Content — Temple & Religious Organization

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Temple & Religious Organization

**Subtitle:** Offerings, Ceremonies, Donors & Accounting with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's temple and religious organization solution helps faith-based NGOs run online merit offerings, ceremony scheduling, memorial dedications, donor records, and recurring sponsorships — with bilingual dedications, counter POS, and accounting in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Online Offerings** — Merit shop for donations, ceremonies, events, and structured dedications.
2. **Donor & Recipient** — Bilingual names, relationships, birth/death dates, and portal quick records.
3. **Ceremony Planning** — Puja and ceremony orders with staff, location, calendar, and reminders.
4. **Recurring Sponsorship** — Memorial tablets, deity lights, subscriptions, and renewal notices.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Merit Web Shop** — Donations, ceremonies, events, and dedication capture at checkout.
2. **Bilingual Registry** — English and local-language recipient and relative records.
3. **Ceremony Ops** — Order-to-ceremony with staff, location, and calendar sync.
4. **Memorial Services** — Tablet and light unit tracking, reservations, and wall grid view.
5. **Offering Slots** — Daily capacity limits, categories, and print-ready fulfillment.
6. **Counter & POS** — Guided wizards, customer screen, and branded receipts.
7. **Donor CRM** — Donor categories, PDPA consent, and portal self-service.
8. **Temple Finance** — Subscriptions, partial invoicing, vouchers, and reconciliation.

## Business Workflow

1. **Register Devotee** — Portal signup with PDPA consent and donor profile.
2. **Choose Offering** — Donations, ceremonies, memorial units, or events online or at counter.
3. **Add Dedication** — Bilingual recipient names, dates, and relationships at checkout.
4. **Plan Ceremony** — Assign staff, location, calendar; print tablets or certificates.
5. **Bill & Engage** — Invoice, subscription renewals, and automated reminders.

## Business Benefits

1. **One Temple Platform** — Website, counter, ceremonies, donors, and finance in one flow.
2. **Accurate Dedications** — Bilingual recipient capture reduces manual re-entry and print errors.
3. **Protected Inventory** — Reservations, subscriptions, and daily slot limits on sacred offerings.
4. **Staff Visibility** — Calendars, dashboards, and reminders for ceremony coordination.
