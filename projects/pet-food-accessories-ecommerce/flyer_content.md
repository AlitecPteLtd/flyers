# Flyer Content — Pet Food & Accessories eCommerce

## Cover / Top Section

**Title:** Pet Food & Accessories eCommerce

**Subtitle:** Premium Online Retail — Loyalty, Bundles & Fulfilment with Odoo

**Introduction:**
Alitec's pet retail eCommerce solution helps premium online stores sell food and accessories, capture pet member profiles, run loyalty and referral programmes, bundle products on the website, and fulfil orders with local shipping — all in one Odoo platform.

## Quick Benefits

1. **Pet Member Profiles** — Register cats and dogs with breed, birthday, and owner details at signup.
2. **Loyalty & Referrals** — Signup points, refer-a-friend codes, and shop redemption at checkout.
3. **Premium Web Store** — Theme Prime storefront with brands, bundles, wishlist, and mobile shopping.
4. **Local Fulfilment** — EasyParcel labels, tracking, and shipping updates after order confirmation.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Pet Member Data** — Pet lines with name, breed, type, and DOB on partners and portal.
2. **Member Registration** — Public signup form with PDPA consent and acquisition source tracking.
3. **Premium eCommerce** — Theme Prime shop with brands, filters, quick view, and comparison.
4. **Product Bundles** — Sell food and accessory kits across web, sales, stock, and invoices.
5. **Loyalty Programmes** — Signup rewards, promotion rules, limits, and multi-product criteria.
6. **Refer & Earn** — Referral codes, friend signup points, and discount redemption online.
7. **EasyParcel Shipping** — Rates, labels, tracking, and post-confirm carrier changes on pickings.
8. **Stripe + PayNow** — Card checkout with Singapore PayNow on the web payment flow.

## Business Workflow

1. **Join & Register** — Member signup with pet profiles, consent, and signup loyalty points.
2. **Shop Online** — Browse brands, bundles, wishlist, and apply loyalty rewards at checkout.
3. **Pay Securely** — Stripe card payment with PayNow for Singapore customers.
4. **Pick & Pack** — Warehouse fulfilment with bundle lines and delivery order creation.
5. **Ship & Track** — EasyParcel labels, tracking links, and branded order documents.
